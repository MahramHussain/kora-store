Main objectives of the Assessment 
 
Assessment 1 is a group activity: students will be allocated into groups of 4 or 5 and given a case study 
to work with throughout the first assessment of module. 
For Assessment 1 you are required to complete: 
• A software project 
• A group report, to be submitted via Blackboard 
Your group report should be written as a collaborative document with contributions from each group 
member, and should provide documentation of the project and software produced by your group, 
including: 
• A literature review demonstrating you have done research on project management and software 
methodologies, and justified the ones you have chosen for your project 
• Evidence of detailed project planning, such as Gantt chart, Work Breakdown Structure, product 
backlog and sprint backlogs, critical path analysis, risk analysis, etc. 
• Requirements analysis for your software project, demonstrating evidence of appropriate 
techniques such as use case diagrams, user stories, etc. 
• Documentation of the design process for your software 
• Evidence of completed software, such as labelled screenshots 
• Evidence of software testing 
• Evidence of teamwork, such as minutes of group meetings, allocation of team roles, evidence of 
shared documents and discussions 
• Evidence of the contribution of each group member to the final project deliverables, including the 
report 
• User documentation for your software, such as a brief user guide 
• Reflection on the project process, what challenges were faced and how you addressed these 
challenges as a group 
• As part of the group-based development to discuss how the key characteristics of Equality 
Diversity and Inclusion (EDI) been reflected on. 
 
Further guidance on how to complete the group report will be provided in due course via teaching 
sessions and/or Blackboard.
